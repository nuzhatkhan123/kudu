Club Sandwich

↓

Step 1: Toast 4 pieces of bread (white or multigrain)

↓

Step 2: Put 7 grams mayo on all slices (28 grams in total), put 35 grams lettuce, smoked chicken and Swiss cheese on one slice, one piece of round tomato, smoked beef, one slice of American cheese on one slice, and smoked turkey and one mashed egg on another piece respectively

↓

Step 4: Stack the bread, turkey over beef over chicken, slice the bread diagonally into 4 partitions, put in box and serve
